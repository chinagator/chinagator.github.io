Mingsong Chen is a full Professor in the Software Engineering Institute (CS&CE) at the East China Normal University (ECNU). Currently he serves as the vice director of the department of Embedded Software and Systems. His research interests include design automation and verification of cyber physical systems, cloud/embedded/parallel computing, computer architecture, and IoT Security. He received his B.S. and M. Eng. from Nanjing University in 2003 and 2006 respectively, and Ph.D. from the University of Florida, in 2010 - all in Computer Science. He is a distinguished member of CCF , a senior member of IEEE, and a member of ACM.
